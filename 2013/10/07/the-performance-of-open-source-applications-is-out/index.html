The Performance of Open Source Applications is out!

Mon 07 Oct 2013 08:00:00

I’m happy to announce that The Performance of Open Source Applications is finally out. You can head over to the AOSA website to read it for free online and find links to places where you can purchase paper or, soon, electronic copies.

If you do purchase it, the royalties go to Amnesty International.

If you want to know more about what the book is about, the introduction is a good place to start. The authors worked hard to share what they know and I learned a lot from them; I hope you do too.
